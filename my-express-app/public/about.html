About Us

Welcome to Interview.io – Your AI-Powered Interview Coach

At Interview.io, we understand the butterflies in your stomach before an important job interview. That's why we've created a unique platform that combines the power of artificial intelligence with the nuanced demands of job interviews, offering a personalized experience to help you shine in your next interview.

Our Story

We are a team of university students, just like you, navigating the challenging terrain of job hunting and internship applications. We know firsthand the nerves that come with preparing for interviews, especially when faced with a variety of roles and industries. That's where Interview.io was born – out of our own need for a comprehensive, yet easy-to-use, interview preparation tool.

What We Offer

Interview.io is more than just a mock interview platform. It's a cutting-edge AI simulator designed to offer custom interview questions tailored to your target job. Here's how it works:

  1. Submit Your Resume:

    Upload your resume, along with the job description and title of the position you're aiming for.

  2. Receive Custom Questions:

    Our AI analyzes your submission and generates interview questions specific to the role you desire.

  3. Practice with Real-Time Feedback:

    Conduct your mock interview verbally. Our AI listens, analyzes your responses, and provides instant feedback on how you can improve – not just what you say, but how you say it.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to empower young graduates and job seekers with the confidence and skills needed to ace their interviews. We believe that practice makes perfect, and real-time feedback is key to mastering the art of interviews.
